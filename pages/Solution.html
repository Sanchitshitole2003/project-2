SOLUTIONS TO CONTROL POLLUTION

Air Pollution

• Use cleaner energy sources like solar, wind, and hydroelectric power.

• Promotr public transportation and carpooling to reduce vehicle emission.

• Enforce stricter emission standards for imdustries and vehicles.

• Plant more trees and crate green spaces to absorb pollutants

Water Pollution

• Properly treat sewage and industrial wastewater before disposal.

• Implement strict regulations on waste discharge into water bodies.

• Promote responsible use of fertilizers and pesticides in agriculture.

• Support initiatiatives to clean up and protect rivers,lakes,and pceans.

Plastic Pollution

• Implement proper hazardous waste management.

• Use organic forming methods to reduce pesticide and chemical use.

• Remediate contaminated sites to prevent further spread.

• Cropping techniques should be improved to prevent growth of weeds.

Soil Pollution

• Design urban planning that separates noisy areas from quiet zones.

• Set noise emission standards for vehicles and machinery.

• Enforce regulations on construction and commercial noise.

• Rules for use of Loudspeakers should be made .
